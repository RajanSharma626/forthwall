Das Studio acknowledge that we live, learn and work on the land of the Kaurna people of the Kaurna Plains, and the Wurundjeri people of the Kulin Nation. We honour the ways their elders past and present have cared for and nurtured their lands and waters for millennia. We pay respect to all Traditional Custodians and Knowledge Holders as keepers of Country and the very first place-makers.
